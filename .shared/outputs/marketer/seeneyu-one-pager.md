# seeneyu — Executive Summary

**The AI-powered body language coaching platform.**
https://seeneyu.vercel.app | by PeeTeeAI

---

## The Problem

55% of communication is body language — yet there is no gym, no Duolingo, no structured practice system for it. Professionals read books, watch TED Talks, and hope to improve. Nobody practices.

## The Solution

seeneyu turns Hollywood's greatest performers into your personal coaching library.

- **Watch** curated movie clips demonstrating a specific skill (eye contact, posture, vocal pacing)
- **Record** yourself mimicking the behavior via webcam
- **Get AI feedback** — scored across multiple dimensions with frame-by-frame analysis
- **Repeat** until the skill becomes muscle memory
