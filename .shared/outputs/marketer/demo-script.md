# seeneyu — 3-Minute Investor Demo Script
> Owner: Marketer | Created: 2026-03-25 | Version: 1.0

---

## Setup
- Open seeneyu.vercel.app on a large screen
- Have webcam ready (you'll do a live recording)
- Pre-select a clip (recommendation: an eye-contact beginner clip — dramatic, clear skill)

---

## [0:00–0:30] THE HOOK

> "Let me ask you something. In your last board meeting, how much of the conversation was about what people said — versus how they said it? How they held their gaze. How they sat. Whether they leaned in or pulled back.
>
> 55% of communication is body language. Everyone knows this. Nobody practices it.
>
> There's Duolingo for Spanish. Peloton for cycling. But until now — no training system for the most important professional skill you use every single day."

---

## [0:30–1:00] THE PRODUCT — WATCH

*Navigate to Library. Click a clip.*

> "This is seeneyu. It's the first AI-powered body language coaching platform.
>
> We start with Hollywood. Every clip in our library is hand-curated — 65 scenes from films like The Pursuit of Happyness, The Devil Wears Prada, A Few Good Men. Each one demonstrates a specific communication skill at a specific difficulty level."

*Play the clip for 5–8 seconds.*

> "See that? [Character name] holds the gaze for exactly 2.3 seconds, then breaks naturally to the right. That's intentional. That's the skill. And now the learner practices it."

---

## [1:00–1:45] THE PRODUCT — RECORD + FEEDBACK

*Click "Practice" or "Record". Show the webcam interface.*

> "The learner turns on their webcam and mimics what they just watched. Our system captures frames during the recording."

*Record yourself for 5–10 seconds doing eye contact.*

> "Behind the scenes, MediaPipe is tracking 478 facial landmarks and full body skeletal mesh. GPT-4o Vision analyzes the frames for posture, gaze direction, facial expression, and gesture quality."

*Show the feedback screen (use a pre-recorded session if live isn't practical).*

> "And here's the magic. Instant, specific, scored feedback. Not 'try better eye contact' — but 'your gaze held for 1.8 seconds at 0:02, aim for 2.5.' Dimensions scored individually. An action plan with specific next steps. This is coaching quality at app scale."

---

## [1:45–2:15] THE MARKET

> "The professional development market is $30 billion. $370 billion is spent on corporate training globally. And the #1 skill employers want — communication — has the worst training tools. Passive videos. No practice. No measurement.
>
> Every competitor in this space — Yoodli, Speeko, Poised — does speech only. Nobody does body language. Nobody has the Watch → Record → AI Feedback loop with visual AI. We're creating this category."

---

## [2:15–2:45] TRACTION + MOAT

> "We're live today. 65 curated clips. 5 skill categories. 3 structured courses with 30 lessons. An Arcade Zone with 30 quick challenges for gamified practice. Full AI feedback pipeline. Built in under 30 days by a lean team.
>
> The data flywheel is the moat. Every recording generates labeled body language data — user video, AI scoring, self-assessment. As we scale, this dataset becomes the foundation for proprietary body language models that no generic AI will match."
