## 8. Why Now — Timing Thesis

1. **Technology unlock**: GPT-4o Vision (May 2024) made real-time body language analysis from video frames commercially viable for the first time. Before this, no model could provide specific, actionable feedback on posture, gaze, and gesture.

2. **Market shift**: Remote/hybrid work forced body language into a tiny video rectangle. The stakes are higher, the margin for error is smaller, and professionals feel the gap.

3. **Category creation**: No product exists in the "active body language practice + AI feedback" category. seeneyu defines it. First-mover advantage in a category with no incumbent.

4. **Proven model**: Duolingo proved that gamified practice loops work for skill-building at scale ($8B+ market cap). seeneyu applies the same model to body language.

5. **Enterprise appetite**: L&D budgets are shifting from passive content to measurable, active skill-building tools. The ROI story for seeneyu (before/after scores, practice completion rates) writes itself.
