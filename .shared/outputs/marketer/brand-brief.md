## Mission Statement

**Empower every professional to master the unspoken language of confident communication — through practice, not theory.**

seeneyu exists because the most important skill in every high-stakes room — body language — has never had a training system. We built the world's first AI coaching platform that turns Hollywood's greatest performances into repeatable, measurable skill-building exercises.

---

## Brand Personality

1. **Confident** — We speak with authority. We've studied the science, built the technology, and watched it work.
2. **Warm** — The amber glow isn't just design. We're approachable, encouraging, never clinical.
3. **Cinematic** — Everything we do borrows from the storytelling craft. We believe great communication is a performance.
4. **Action-Oriented** — We don't just teach, we make you practice. Every interaction moves the learner forward.
5. **Precise** — AI feedback is specific, not vague. "Your gaze dropped at 0:04" beats "try better eye contact."

## Target Personas

### Persona 1: The Ambitious Professional (Learner)
- **Who**: 25–40, knowledge worker, hybrid/remote. UX designers, product managers, salespeople, consultants.
- **Pain**: Knows body language matters. Has read the articles. Never practiced. Feels stiff on camera. Wants to project confidence but doesn't know how.
- **Motivation**: Career advancement, interview prep, presentation skills, dating confidence.
- **Behavior**: Uses Duolingo, watches TED Talks, subscribes to self-improvement content. Would pay $10–15/mo for something that measurably works.

### Persona 2: The L&D Buyer (B2B Decision-Maker)
- **Who**: 35–55, Head of Learning & Development, HR Director, VP Sales Enablement.
- **Pain**: Spends budget on passive video courses. No measurable ROI on soft skills training. Employees complete courses and forget everything.
- **Motivation**: Reduce onboarding time, improve team presentation skills, measurable skill scores to report to leadership.
- **Behavior**: Buys Udemy Business, LinkedIn Learning, Coursera for Business. Wants SCORM/LMS integration, usage analytics, and proof of improvement.

## Tone of Voice

### We Sound Like...
- A world-class acting coach who also happens to be a data scientist
- Encouraging but never patronizing
- Specific and actionable, never vague
- Confident without arrogance

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Confident** | "You'll master eye contact in 2 weeks" | "We hope this might help with eye contact" |
| **Warm** | "Great start — here's what to refine" | "Score: 45/100. Try harder." |
| **Precise** | "Your gaze held for 1.8s — aim for 2.5s" | "Your eye contact needs work" |
| **Active** | "Record yourself. Get feedback. Repeat." | "Consider recording yourself when convenient" |
| **Cinematic** | "Watch how Denzel holds the room" | "This video shows good body language" |

### Formatting Rules
- Use em-dashes — not hyphens — for dramatic pauses in copy
- Numbers: spell out one through nine, use digits for 10+
- Skill names are always lowercase hyphenated: `eye-contact`, `vocal-pacing`
- "seeneyu" is always lowercase, even at the start of a sentence
- "AI" is always capitalized. "AI-powered" is hyphenated.

## Key Messages

### What We Always Say
- Body language is a **skill**, not a talent — it can be practiced and improved
- seeneyu uses **real-world performances** as training material, not staged examples
- AI feedback is **instant, specific, and personalized** — not generic tips
- The Watch → Record → Feedback loop creates **muscle memory**, not just knowledge
- 65+ curated scenes from Hollywood's most compelling performers
- 5 essential communication skills: eye contact, posture, listening, vocal pacing, confident disagreement

### What We Never Say
- "Fake it till you make it" — we teach authentic skill, not deception
- "Body language tricks" — we don't do tricks, we build habits
- "Read people's body language" — we're about **performing**, not interpreting others
- "Guaranteed results" — we coach practice, outcomes depend on effort
- Never compare to "manipulation" or "influence hacks"
- Never claim to replace therapy, coaching, or medical advice
