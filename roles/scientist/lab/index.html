Mission Statement

This lab documents the scientific evidence that the seeneyu core loop is grounded in established learning science. We research both social science (psychological and behavioral frameworks) and clinical science (empirical intervention studies). All sources are graded by evidence quality.

Evidence Grade Legend

Grade Definitions

Grade A Meta-Analysis Systematic review or meta-analysis of multiple controlled studies. Highest evidence tier.
Grade B Controlled Study Randomized controlled trial or peer-reviewed experimental study with comparison group.
Grade C Cohort / Observational Longitudinal cohort, observational study, or non-randomized intervention study.
Grade D Expert Framework Influential theoretical framework, expert textbook, or widely cited conceptual review.
