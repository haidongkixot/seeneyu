## Slide 6: The Technology Behind the Magic

### Privacy-First AI Analysis

Your video **never leaves your device.** seeneyu uses Google's MediaPipe — the same technology used by Instagram filters — to analyze your facial expressions, body posture, and hand gestures **directly in your browser.**

### What We Analyze (In Real-Time)

- **52 facial expression points** — every micro-movement of your eyes, eyebrows, mouth, and cheeks
- **11 body landmarks** — shoulder openness, spine alignment, forward lean, head tilt
- **21 hand landmarks per hand** — gesture openness, finger spread, positioning
- **Voice analysis** — pitch variety, speaking rate, pause patterns, volume dynamics
- **Temporal patterns** — transition smoothness, expression holds, rhythm and flow

### The Result: A Holistic Score

Not just "you scored 75." But exactly *where* you're strong, *where* you need work, and *what to do next* — across face, body, hands, voice, and timing.
